About the Project

Where Heritage
Meets Elevation

Michael Apartment CHS Ltd. is undergoing a transformative redevelopment on P.K. Road, Mulund West — one of Mumbai's most sought-after established suburbs. Designed by M/S. Speco Architect (Ar. Nitin Gunjal), the tower rises with curvaceous architectural lines, a dramatic podium base, and a rooftop swimming pool — setting a new benchmark for luxury living in Mulund.


With dedicated commercial spaces on the lower floors, curated amenities on an exclusive amenity deck, and generously proportioned 2 & 3 BHK residences above, Michael Apartment is designed for families who demand more from their address.

📈 Consistent Appreciation
Mulund West averaged ₹34,500/sq.ft in Q1 2025, up from ₹33,800 the prior year — confirming sustained buyer confidence and steady capital appreciation in the micro-market.
🚇 Metro & Infrastructure Boost
Proposed Goregaon–Mulund Link Road (GMLR) and Sonapur Metro Station are expected to significantly improve connectivity and drive demand for Mulund West addresses.
🏙️ Redevelopment Momentum
Over 55% of 2024 Mumbai property registrations occurred in suburbs including Mulund. Redevelopment projects fetch 1.5× older home prices, making Michael Apartment a compelling proposition.
💼 Strong Rental Demand
Monthly rents in Mulund West range ₹29,000–₹1.3 lakh depending on unit size, supported by proximity to Thane and Navi Mumbai employment hubs and excellent road connectivity.
